PM-Kisan Samman Nidhi

The PM-Kisan Samman Nidhi (Pradhan Mantri Kisan Samman Nidhi) is a Central Sector scheme by the Government of India. It provides financial assistance to small and marginal farmers to help them meet their agricultural and domestic needs.
